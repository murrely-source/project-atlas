# Solaris Executive AI Collaboration Framework

> **Working Draft v0.1**

## Purpose

The Solaris Executive AI Collaboration Framework is a governance
methodology for collaborating with AI. Rather than focusing only on
prompt engineering techniques, it defines a repeatable process for
producing trustworthy, reviewable, and well-governed AI-assisted work.

## Philosophy

**Technology for Human-Centered AI**

AI should amplify human judgment---not replace it. Prompting is one tool
within a broader collaboration process that emphasizes governance,
documentation, validation, and executive decision support.

## Core Principles

-   Human judgment remains essential.
-   Context is as important as the prompt.
-   Constraints improve quality.
-   Governance enables trustworthy outcomes.
-   Documentation creates institutional knowledge.
-   Review and refinement are expected.
-   Approved work becomes the new source of truth.

## Collaboration Lifecycle

1.  Define Purpose
2.  Establish Context
3.  Define Constraints
4.  Confirm Understanding
5.  Execute
6.  Review
7.  Challenge
8.  Refine
9.  Approve
10. Document
11. Govern
12. Reuse

## Relationship to Prompt Engineering

Traditional prompt engineering techniques---including zero-shot,
one-shot, few-shot, chain-of-thought, role prompting, and structured
prompting---are valuable tools. Within the Solaris framework, they are
applied inside a larger governance lifecycle rather than being treated
as the end goal.

## Executive Questions

-   What decision are we trying to support?
-   What context already exists?
-   What must not change?
-   How will the result be validated?
-   How will this become reusable organizational knowledge?

## Vision

Solaris Labs will continue refining this framework into a formal
methodology supporting executive AI collaboration, product development,
governance, and organizational adoption.
